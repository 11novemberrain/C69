Favourite Destination

Singapore Skyline

Singapore from the skies

Singapore has been one of my favourite overseas destination ever since I was in College. With my sister moving there early on, I jumped at the opportunity to stay with her on multiple occasions and experience the best that the City has to offer. From great transport connectivity, grand shopping malls to majestic sky scrappers, Singapore has it all, in its self contained tiny dot.



Downtown Chicago

Chicago - The best Summer and downtown fun!

Chicago continues to be one of my favourite cities to live in, despite experiencing 9 months of extreme winter. The city makes up for its snowy winter with an even more exhuberant summer with various actvities at the Millennium Park, shopping along Michigan Ave, or taking boat rides along the Chicago river.
